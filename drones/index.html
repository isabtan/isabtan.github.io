Designed and developed by Isabelle Tan, drones is a series of generative artworks featuring the waveforms of multiple oscillators. Each composition features a unique color palette, sinewave, and frequency range. The project is inspired by John De Cesare’s Graphic Scores & Notations, György Ligeti’s Artikulation, Floating Points’ Falaise, Aphex Twin's Peek 82454201, and Black Devil Disco Club’s « H » Friend.
