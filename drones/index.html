Designed, developed, and then bound by Isabelle Tan, drones is a series of generative artworks featuring the waveforms of multiple oscillators. Each composition
features a unique color palette, sinewave, and frequency range. The project is inspired by John De Cesare’s Graphic Scores & Notations, György Ligeti’s Artikulation,
Floating Points’ Falaise, and Four Tet’s Ribbons.
